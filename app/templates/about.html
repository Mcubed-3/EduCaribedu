About the platform

Helping teachers plan faster without sacrificing quality

EduCarib AI is a lesson planning platform created to support Caribbean educators with structured, curriculum-aligned lesson plans and classroom-ready activities. It is designed to reduce planning time while keeping lessons practical, editable, and useful in real classrooms.

What EduCarib AI does

Built for real teaching workflow

  • Generate structured lesson plans using formats such as 5Es and 4Cs
  • Create classroom-ready activities including quizzes, worksheets, exit tickets, and homework sheets
  • Support multiple subjects, grade levels, and curriculum needs
  • Allow teachers to edit generated lesson plans and activities before exporting
  • Provide export-ready PDF and DOCX outputs for classroom use
Why it matters

Less time formatting. More time teaching.

Teachers already carry a heavy workload. EduCarib AI helps reduce the time spent building lesson plans from scratch, while still giving teachers control to edit, adjust, and personalise the final output for their students.


The goal is not to replace teacher judgment. The goal is to make planning faster, cleaner, and more manageable.

Our mission

Modern tools for Caribbean educators

EduCarib AI exists to give teachers in the Caribbean access to modern planning tools that feel practical, polished, and relevant. The platform is focused on helping educators save time, improve consistency, and produce stronger teaching materials with less friction.
