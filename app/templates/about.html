About EduCarib AI

EduCarib AI is a lesson planning platform designed specifically for Caribbean educators. It helps teachers quickly generate structured, curriculum-aligned lesson plans and classroom activities.

What We Do

  • Generate structured lesson plans (5E, 4C formats)
  • Create clean classroom-ready activities
  • Support multiple subjects and grade levels
  • Provide export-ready DOCX and PDF outputs

Why EduCarib?

Teachers spend hours planning. EduCarib reduces that time to minutes while still keeping quality, structure, and curriculum alignment.

Our Goal

To empower Caribbean educators with modern tools that improve teaching efficiency and student outcomes.
