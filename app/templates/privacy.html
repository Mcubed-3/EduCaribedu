Privacy Policy

EduCarib AI is committed to handling user data responsibly and transparently.

Information we collect

We may collect account details such as your email address, authentication data, lesson content you generate or save, feedback submissions, and limited billing-related account information needed to provide the service.

How we use information

We use data to operate the platform, authenticate users, improve lesson and activity generation, provide support, review bug reports and feedback, and manage subscriptions and billing access.

Cookies and sessions

EduCarib AI uses session cookies to keep users signed in and maintain secure access to the platform.

Advertising and third-party services

EduCarib AI may use Google AdSense and related advertising technologies. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this website or other websites.

Users may be able to opt out of personalized advertising through Google’s ad settings and related controls offered by Google.

Payments

Paid subscriptions are managed through third-party payment providers such as Stripe. EduCarib AI does not store full card details on its own servers.

Data retention

We retain user content, saved lessons, and support submissions for as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve the platform.

User rights

Users may request updates or deletion of their account-related data where reasonably possible, subject to operational and legal requirements.

Children

This platform is intended for teachers, educators, and other adult users. It is not designed as a direct service for children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after updates means the revised policy applies.

Contact

For privacy questions, users can contact EduCarib AI through the feedback and support page available within the platform.
